ONLINE RETAIL POWER BI DASHBOARD

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard built using an Online Retail dataset.
The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, customer behavior, and geographic performance,
helping businesses make data-driven decisions.

DATASET DESCRIPTION :
Dataset: Online Retail Dataset- Invoice numbers- Product descriptions- Quantity sold- Unit price & revenue- Customer IDs- Country-wise transactions

OBJECTIVES- Analyze overall revenue performance- Identify top customers and countries- Understand monthly sales trends- Examine product demand and quantity sold- Visualize global sales distribution

DASHBOARD FEATURES :
Revenue Trend Analysis – Median revenue by month (2011)
Top Customers – Top 10 customers by revenue
Country-wise Performance – Quantity vs revenue comparison
Global Sales Map – Units sold across countries

PROJECT STRUCTURE :
Online-Retail-PowerBI
OnlineRetail.pbix
dataset.csv

KEY INSIGHTS :
Peak revenue observed around May and late summer months
European countries contribute significantly to sales
A small group of customers drives most revenue
High quantity does not always mean high revenue
Sales drop toward the end of the year
